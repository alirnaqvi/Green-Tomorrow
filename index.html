Join us in making a difference! Khalq Organization is organizing a sustainability event at Islamabad Model College for Boys,

Our Mission

Our goal is to engage students and the community in efforts focused on:

  • SDG 11: Sustainable Cities and Communities
  • SDG 13: Climate Action
  • SDG 15: Life on Land
